================================================================
   INSTALL JEWELRY INVOICE PRO ON YOUR PHONE
================================================================

This app works on Android (Samsung, etc.) and iPhone!


========================================
STEP 1: HOST THE APP ONLINE
========================================

You need to put the app online first. Easy free options:

Option A: GitHub Pages (Free)
1. Go to your GitHub repository
2. Go to Settings > Pages
3. Select "Deploy from a branch"
4. Select "main" branch and click Save
5. Your app will be at: https://yourusername.github.io/Jewelery-Invoicing-app1/

Option B: Netlify (Free)
1. Go to https://netlify.com
2. Sign up / Log in
3. Drag and drop your project folder
4. Get your free URL


========================================
STEP 2: INSTALL ON SAMSUNG S23 (Android)
========================================

1. Open Chrome on your phone

2. Go to your app URL (from Step 1)

3. Wait for the page to fully load

4. Tap the 3 dots menu (top right corner)

5. Tap "Add to Home screen" or "Install app"

6. Tap "Install" on the popup

7. The app icon will appear on your home screen!

8. Open it - it works like a regular app!


========================================
STEP 2 (Alternative): INSTALL ON iPHONE
========================================

1. Open Safari on your iPhone

2. Go to your app URL (from Step 1)

3. Tap the Share button (square with arrow)

4. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"

5. Tap "Add"

6. The app icon will appear on your home screen!


========================================
FEATURES ON PHONE
========================================

- Works offline after first load
- Full screen (no browser bar)
- Saves data locally on your phone
- Can export PDFs
- Updates automatically when you update the website


========================================
TROUBLESHOOTING
========================================

"Add to Home screen" not showing?
- Make sure you're using Chrome (Android) or Safari (iPhone)
- The page must be fully loaded
- Try refreshing the page

App not updating?
- Open the app, pull down to refresh
- Or clear app data in phone settings

Data not saving?
- Don't use private/incognito mode
- Allow cookies/local storage
